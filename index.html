The Cameroonian Association for the Well-Bieng of Families

Founded in 2005 , ACBF is a Non-Gouvermental Organisation that deals with Sponsoring vulnerable children, Orphans , children who are willing to learn but don't have financial assisatnce of all background in Cameroon.

At ACBF, we believe in the power of collective action to transform lives and create a brighter future for children.Ever since we started, we've been all about helping by working together with communities, schools, and parents for the wellfare of Kids.

Our Aim

At ACBF we provide :


Placement for children in local establishments

Advices in guidance service for your children

Placements in vocational training centres

Insured and financed school year

What sets us apart

What makes us different is our strong belief in the importance of children's success and families' well-being. We think that when a child smiles, it spreads happiness to families, communities, and even the whole country.

Our NGO stands out because we don't just focus on one thing. We help kids with education and health, and we also support families with things they need to live better lives. This way, we help not only kids but also their families and the whole community.

We also want you to know that we're honest and responsible with the help we get. We use it wisely to make real and lasting changes. When you choose us, you're joining a group of people who believe in the power of a child's smile to make the world a better place for everyone.
